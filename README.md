## 💎 The Musicify Experience

We believe that listening to music should be more than just a background activity. Musicify is built on four pillars of excellence:

### 🎨 1. Aesthetic Excellence (Design System)
Don't settle for "standard." Musicify features a state-of-the-art design system that adapts to your mood. Choose from over **10+ handcrafted visual identities**, including:
*   **Midnight Gold**: Deep blacks with elegant gold accents.
*   **Rose Pine**: Soft, serene tones for late-night sessions.
*   **Cyberpunk**: High-contrast, neon-infused energy.
*   **Nord & Oceanic**: Calm, professional, and easy on the eyes.
*   **Classic Dark/Light**: Refined versions of your favorite modes.

### 📜 2. Precision Interaction
Engage with your music on a deeper level:
*   **Interactive Lyrics**: Follow every word with beautiful, synchronized scrolling. **Click any line** to jump instantly to that moment in the song.
*   **Smart Mini Player**: A sleek, always-on-top overlay that lets you control your audio while staying productive. Perfect for work or deep focus.
*   **Fluid Animations**: Every transition is designed to be smooth and responsive, creating a "living" interface.

### 🔍 3. Seamless Discovery & Control
Unlock a world of audio without boundaries:
*   **Universal Search**: Find any track, album, or artist instantly across a global music index.
*   **Offline Library**: Keep your favorite tracks close. Save them to your local library for high-quality playback even when you're disconnected.
*   **Intelligent Organization**: Your library organizes itself. No more manual tagging or messy folders.

### 📊 4. Personal Insights
Musicify understands your habits. Get a bird's-eye view of your listening journey with **Detailed Stats**:
*   Track your most-played songs and favorite artists.
*   Monitor your total listening time.
*   Explore your musical evolution through visual charts.

## 📥 Installation

Ready to upgrade your listening? Download the latest version for your platform below.

### 🪟 Windows
1.  Visit the [**Latest Release**](https://github.com/MTcong/musicify-releases/releases/latest) page.
2.  Download the `Musicify-Setup-x.x.x.exe` file.
3.  Launch and enjoy.

### 🍎 macOS
1.  Download the `Musicify-x.x.x.dmg` file.
2.  Drag **Musicify** to your **Applications** folder.
3.  *First launch: Right-click and select "Open" to authorize.*

### 🐧 Linux
1.  Download the `Musicify-x.x.x.AppImage`.
2.  Make it executable (`chmod +x`) and run.

## 💬 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a subscription fee?**
A: No. Musicify is a passion project built for the community. Enjoy the full experience without hidden costs or account requirements.

**Q: How do I manage my local collection?**
A: Simply use the "Search" feature to find tracks and hit the "Add to Library" button. Musicify handles the rest, ensuring your collection is always high-quality and well-organized.

**Q: My lyrics aren't showing up correctly, what should I do?**
A: While in the Lyrics view, click the **"Search & Fix"** icon. You can manually select the perfect match from our global database to ensure your experience is flawless.

**Q: Does Musicify support multiple languages?**
A: Absolutely. We currently support **10+ languages** including English, Vietnamese, German, Spanish, French, and more. You can switch instantly in the Settings.
